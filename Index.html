The Perfect PB&J

Description:

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

    Ingredients:

  • Two slices of white bread
  • One jar of grape jelly
  • One jar of peanut butter
  • One butter knife
  • One cutting board

    Directions:

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other.
  2. Spread peanut butter on the left slice of bread.
  3. Spread jelly on the right slice of bread.
  4. Carefully place the two halves together.
